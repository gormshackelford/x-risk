Existential Risk (X-Risk)

Existential risk is the risk of human extinction or the collapse of human civilization.

The Existential Risk Research Assessment (TERRA)

Using crowdsourcing and machine learning, we produce an open-access bibliography of publications about existential risk and global catastrophic risk. Please see our publication in Futures for more information.
